Big Cat Rescue

Problem

How can we redesign Big Cat Rescue to help retain and expand their membership base?

Objective

Big Cat Rescue should educate their users about their mission in a manner that would convince visitors to donate towards the cause.

Solution

The site was redesigned to convey a vibrant and exotic style to all animal lovers. With an attractive interface and fun new interactions, itâ€™s a surefire way to increase site traffic and donations.

Role

UI/UX Designer, Graphic Designer, Design System Lead

Tools

Figma, Photoshop

Timeline

3 Weeks

User Research

We conducted surveys and user interviews and learned that our typical user was between 30-35 and a likely pet owner.
They have donated in the past and prefer to be referred by a close friend or relative.
They would only like an annual update after donating and organizational transparency is critical.

Definition

User Goals

  • Feel they can trust the organization
  • Know how their donations will be used
  • Want more payment options to donate
  • Avoid information overload

Feature Priorities

  • Option to pay via Venmo, Zelle or Credit Card
  • Option to donate once or monthly
  • Offer newsletter subscription
  • Interactive way for users to learn about the type of cats in the sanctuary
